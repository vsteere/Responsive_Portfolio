About Me

I am a finance professional with six years of experience working for large government organizations including Broward County, Florida, City of Rowlett, Texas, and City of Grand Prairie, Texas. In addition to my extensive finance and budget skills I also have experience implementing ERP software solutions including Oracle PeopleSoft Finance and Planning and Budgeting Cloud Solution (PBCS). I was the subject matter expert and designed both user forms and workflows as part of the PBCS implementation. I have extensive experience with the full software development lifecycle and both waterfall and agile software implementations



During the summer of 2020 I enrolled in Southern Methodist University's web development boot camp and am currently learning to design, develop, and deploy websites using the Javascript MERN stack. Other items included in the boot camp include Search Engine Optimization, and various technologies to complement HTML and Javscript website development.
